SUMMARY

Since the year of 2020, during the pandemic and my 3rd year in junior high school, I have been a hobbyist game developer and pixel artist making games and artworks in the Philippines.
After discovering pixel art, I started as a freelance pixel artist since 2021 up until 2024.
As 2024 approached the year end, I then became a full-time Game Artist at Bitrograde under Mastiff LLC, also known as Mastiff Systems.

When I started making games, these were usually for game hackathons known as game jams.
I've made over 10 games that were made during a span of 48 hours up until a week depending on the jam's timeframe.
These also include a few capstone projects I made for school.

Alongside my game projects, I was also creating pixel artworks to further hone my art skills in the pixel art style.
I started from making small game mockups and sprites, to full scenes or character portraits.
